Why Work at Om Sai Trade?

Job opportunities within Om Sai Trade extend across each of our verticals and offer the chance to work with large commercial and governmental organizations on projects that really matter.

As a Om Sai Trade employee, you’ll not only have the ability to work with some of the market’s leading organizations but will also become part of a team comprising experienced leaders in power backup and telecommunication equipment supply. Senior Human Resource Specialist (Resource Executive/Lead):

Number of Positions: 1

Experience: 1 – 3 Years

Education Qualifications: MBA/Masters in Computers or Art’s (English)

 

Required Skills:
         Good oral and written communication skills in English.

  •          Proficiency in MS Office (Word, Excel and PowerPoint) is mandatory.
  •          Hands on internet browsing skills
  •          Minimum 12 Months of prior Sales/Marketing experience is mandatory.
  •          Able to work in the night shift, which should match US Eastern Standard Time Zone
  •          Self-Motivated, Innovative and Passion to make career in US Staffing

Job Responsibilities:

  • Lead a team of Jr. Resource Executives
  • Gathering and understanding requirements from Resource/Account Manager
  • Prioritize requirements and assign them to Jr. Resource Executives
  • Helping team to prioritize skills, keywords, create search criteria and strings.
  • Help Jr.  Resource Executives in searching resumes from Job Sites/Portals/Boards and Social Media
  • Check the format of shortlisted resumes and submit to Account / Resource Manager
  • Follow-up on submissions and scheduling interviews for shortlisted candidates
  • Building and developing relationships with people in appropriate skills.
  • Creating a referral network, as well as setting goals and creating plans for self-improvement are traits of a successful Recruiter.
